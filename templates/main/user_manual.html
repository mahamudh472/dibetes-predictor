Introduction

Welcome to the GlucoGuide User Manual. This guide will help you navigate the features of GlucoGuide, a tool designed to assist in managing diabetes.

System Requirements

GlucoGuide is compatible with modern browsers and mobile devices. Ensure you have an internet connection for optimal performance.

Getting Started

To start using GlucoGuide, click the "Get started" button in the navigation bar. This will redirect you to the login page. If you don't have an account, click "Register now" at the bottom of the login form to go to the Sign-Up page. Complete the registration form, including Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), to create your account. After registering, you’ll be redirected to a health survey form. Once you've completed the survey, your account will be ready to use.

Key Features

1. Daily Health Calendar

Update your blood pressure and glucose levels daily in the Health Calendar. You can find the calendar in the navigation menu. If your height and weight are not yet added to your profile, you’ll need to update them. Go to your profile page, click "Edit Profile," and enter your height and weight in the designated fields. Afterward, return to the calendar, where the Health Information fields will now be available. On the left side, you’ll see a color-coded calendar indicating the selected date, and on the right side are fields for entering daily health information. Be sure to fill it out each day.

2. Diabetes Prediction

Diabetes prediction is performed by a trained model, which automatically processes data in the background whenever you update your daily Health Calendar form. The model monitors your daily health information and retrieves necessary details from your profile. The prediction results will be displayed on the Prediction page, which you can access by clicking "Prediction" in the navigation menu.

3. Dietary Suggestions

Dietary suggestions are generated after the prediction model completes its analysis and returns the results. Based on the report, dietary recommendations are made according to predefined conditions that depend on the diabetes risk percentage. You can find these dietary suggestions on the Dietary Suggestions page.

Troubleshooting

If you experience login issues, click "Forgot Password" to reset your credentials. For further assistance, contact support.
